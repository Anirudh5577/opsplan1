Ops Plan Overview

By Anirudh Kodiyattil Sreekumar

Reason for Plan

The plan ensures effective help desk operations for this fiscal year.

Mission

Provide seamless and efficient service, improving customer satisfaction.

Vision

Build streamlined processes to support growth and innovation.

SMART Goals

  • Goal 1: Improve first call resolution rate by 10%.
  • Goal 2: Achieve 90% SLA compliance within this year.
  • Goal 3: Reduce ticket resolution time to under 24 hours.
  • Goal 4: Increase agent training hours to 12 per month.
  • Goal 5: Maintain 95% customer satisfaction consistently.

User Benefits

  • Faster ticket resolution improves user efficiency.
  • Consistent support ensures minimal downtime.
  • Skilled agents provide accurate problem-solving.
  • Reliable service enhances trust in help desk operations.
  • Improved customer experience increases satisfaction.
